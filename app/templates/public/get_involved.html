Get Involved

Volunteer Opportunities

Are you interested in volunteering? Do you have tech skills and want to share those with the community? Do you have other skills that aren't necessarily tech skills that can still help? If so, we'd be happy to have you. There are no time commitments for volunteering. Volunteer as little or as much as you would like.

If you are interested in volunteering for the first time, we will invite you to attend our next bi-monthly volunteer get together and training. This is an optional event for our existing volunteers, but we highly encourage everyone who volunteers to attend to meet new volunteers and socialize and get to know our other volunteers better.

To keep our community safe and healthy we ask that all volunteers wear masks when attending in-person events. This helps make sure that we are not still contributing to the spread of COVID and other communicable diseases that are harmful and dangerous to our immunocompromised and disabled community members.

Partner With Us

Why Partner With Us?

  • You will be helping bridge the digital divide here in Portland, Oregon.
  • You will be supporting equitable access to technology and digital literacy.
  • You will be able to enhance your organization's community impact and visibility.

Recognition for Our Partners

If you partner with us, we will highlight our partnership in a number of ways.

  • Your logo and name will be featured on our website and event materials
  • Your partnership with us will be highlighted in newsletters and/or social media
  • There may be opportunities for co-branding on events or initiatives

How You Can Partner With Us

  1. Provide Financial Support
    • Sponsor specific initiatives, like tech classes or aid funds.
    • Help cover operational costs, such as class venue rentals or device repairs.
  3. Donate Equipment or Supplies
    • Provide refurbished devices, software licenses, tools, or other tech-related resources
    • Reduce e-waste by channeling surplus technology to those in need
  5. Offer Expertise
    • Collaborate on tech-related events or classes with your staff's expertise
    • Share knowledge and resources to enhance our programs
  7. Collaborate on Outreach
    • Help promote our services to your network
    • Refer individuals who may benefit from our tech support or classes
  9. Provide Space
    • Allow us to use your facilities for hosting classes, events, or drop-in tech support
