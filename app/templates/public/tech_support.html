Get Tech Support

How to Get Support

We offer a few different ways to get support with your tech.

  • In-Person
  • Get in person support during our drop-in hours. We regularly post updated times and locations for where we have our drop-in tech support. We try to have at least two a month. We prefer if you let us know before hand if you're coming, but if that's not possible, we are happy to help if you just drop by.
  • In-Home
  • Get in home support if you aren't able to get to a in person drop-in. If your network isn't working, your desktop is too big to lug around, or you're just unable to make it due to a disability or unreliable transportation, we will work with you to schedule a time for one of our volunteers to come to your home to help out.
  • Over the Phone
  • Have a simple issue that you don't mind getting walked through solving? We are more than happy to get on a phone call with you to troubleshoot your issues.

We offer a wide range of services, including basic software and hardware troubleshooting, virus removal, device and network setup, basic data recovery, hardware repairs (if possible), device tune-ups, data backups and transfers, hardware installation, and more. If you’re unsure whether we can help with your specific issue, please feel free to reach out and ask.

We support computers (laptops or desktops), smartphones, tablets, and home networking devices. We don’t typically work on items like gaming consoles or cameras, but if you need help with a unique device, it doesn’t hurt to ask—we may have a volunteer who can assist.
